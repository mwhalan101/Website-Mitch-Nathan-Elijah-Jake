Tyrannosaurus

Quick Info:

  • Height:15 – 20 ft
  • Mass: 13,000 – 32,000 lbs
  • Type of animal: Dinosour/reptile
  • Current status: Extinct

Detail:

  • A very large bipedal carnivorous dinosaur of the late Cretaceous period, with powerful jaws and small clawlike front legs.
  • Tyrannosaurus rex had small arms that were extremely powerful and featured two clawed fingers
  • Tyrannosaurus rex was one of the largest land carnivores of all time
  • The largest known Tyrannosaurus rex skull measures up to 1.52 meters in length.
