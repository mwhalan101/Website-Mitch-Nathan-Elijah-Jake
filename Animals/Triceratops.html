Triceratops

Quick Info:

  • Height: 2.9 – 3 m
  • Mass: 5,900 – 12,000 kg

Detail:

  • The largest known Triceratops skull is estimated to have been 2.5 metres in length
  • Triceratops species possessed a sturdy build, with strong limbs, short hands with three hooves each, and short feet with four hooves each.
